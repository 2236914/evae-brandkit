📄 Part 2 — Platform Documentation

1. System Overview

Collagen IQ™ is an AI-powered skin analysis platform built for aesthetic practitioners and their patients. In simple terms: a practitioner photographs a patient's skin using the iOS app, the AI analyses the images in seconds, and the system produces a detailed collagen health score, zone-by-zone breakdown, and a personalized treatment plan — all of which can be securely shared with the patient through their own app experience.

The platform is built as a multi-tenant SaaS system — meaning multiple clinics can each use the platform independently, with their patient data kept completely separate and secure. Think of it like how each restaurant on Uber Eats manages its own menu and orders — all on the same platform, but fully isolated from each other.

2. Layer-by-Layer Explanation

The platform is organized into four stacked layers, each with a specific role. Data flows downward through the layers during a scan, and back upward to display results.

📱

Presentation Layer — The iOS App

Everything the practitioner and patient actually see and touch. This includes the scan camera, results screens, treatment plan view, progress charts, report sharing, and the AI Doctor Avatar. It is the face of the platform — clean, clinical, and designed for both speed and clarity.

↓ Sends actions to the Business Logic Layer

⚙️

Business Logic Layer — The Brain

The engine that processes everything behind the scenes. It manages the scan session lifecycle (from capture to complete), runs images through the AI analysis pipeline, calculates zone and layer scores, generates treatment plans, builds PDF reports, and enforces subscription limits. Nothing happens without this layer deciding it should.

↓ Reads and writes to the Data Layer · ↑ Sends results to Presentation

🗄️

Data Layer — The Memory

A structured PostgreSQL database (via Supabase) that stores everything permanently: patient records, scan sessions, images, AI analysis results, treatment plans, reports, consent records, and audit logs. Every piece of data is tagged with a clinic ID so that no clinic can ever see another clinic's information — enforced at the database level, not just in the app.

↓ Connected to Infrastructure · ↑ Provides data to Business Logic

☁️

Infrastructure Layer — The Foundation

The external services the platform runs on: the AI model that analyses images, cloud storage for patient photos, real-time updates (WebSocket) for the processing screen, email and push notifications, the authentication system, Stripe for payments, and the CDN that delivers the AI Doctor Avatar video. These are proven, enterprise-grade services — not built from scratch.

↑ Powers the Data and Business Logic layers

🔐

Security & Compliance — The Shield

Running alongside all layers is a dedicated security system: row-level data isolation, encrypted storage, MFA for practitioners, audit logs recording every action, GDPR consent tracking, and the token-based sharing system that lets patients access results securely without a password. This is not optional — it is built into the architecture from the start.

⟺ Wraps all other layers

3. Core Flow — From Scan to Patient

Here is exactly what happens, step by step, when a practitioner scans a patient and shares their results.

1

Practitioner opens the patient profile and taps "New Scan"

The app confirms patient consent is on record before allowing the scan to begin. A new session record is created in the database with a status of "pending".

DB: INSERT sessions (status = 'pending')
2

Camera captures 5 standardized photos

The app guides the practitioner through 5 shots — front face, left profile, right profile, neck, and hands. An alignment guide and lighting indicator assist with quality. Each image is uploaded to secure cloud storage immediately after capture.

DB: INSERT images ×5 · Storage: encrypted upload
3

Practitioner taps "Analyse Now" — AI processing begins

The AI Analysis Engine receives the 5 images and begins scoring. The session status updates to "processing". The practitioner sees an animated processing screen with live stage updates — no manual refresh needed (powered by real-time WebSocket).

DB: UPDATE sessions (status = 'processing')
4

AI produces the Collagen IQ Score and full analysis

The AI engine scores 6 collagen layers and 6 facial zones, calculates the overall Collagen IQ score (0–100), estimates the biological skin age, and builds the deficit index. All results are written to the analyses table.

DB: INSERT analyses (total_score, layer_scores, zone_scores, deficit_index)
5

App auto-redirects to Score Results — practitioner reviews

The full analysis is displayed: the score gauge, age comparison, radar chart, zone map, and collagen deficit bars. The AI Doctor Avatar explains the results in plain language, as if presenting to a colleague. The practitioner can tap into any zone for a deeper breakdown.

DB: READ analyses + zone_scores + layer_scores
6

Treatment Plan is generated and customized

The AI suggests a ranked list of treatments based on the worst-scoring zones and deficit types. The practitioner can drag to reorder, add or remove treatments, attach a skincare protocol, and write their own clinical notes. The plan can be saved as a reusable template.

DB: INSERT treatment_plans · INSERT treatment_items
7

PDF Report is generated and shared with the patient

A branded PDF is generated covering the score, zone map, treatment plan, and clinical notes. The practitioner taps "Share with patient" — a unique secure link is created that expires in 24 hours. The patient receives this link by email.

DB: INSERT reports (share_token, expires_at)
8

Patient opens their secure link — views results in plain language

The patient sees their Collagen IQ score, age comparison, treatment plan, and progress — all in plain, encouraging language with no clinical jargon. They give digital consent before any data is shown. They can optionally set a password to return later. The AI Doctor Avatar explains their results in a friendly, accessible way.

DB: INSERT consent_records · READ analyses (patient-scoped)

4. Key Features

The platform is built around five core capabilities that make it distinct from any general analytics tool.

🧬

AI Collagen Analysis

5 photographs analyzed by a trained AI model that scores 6 collagen layers, 6 facial zones, 5 collagen types, and produces a single 0–100 Collagen IQ score with biological age estimation. Results in under 60 seconds.

⚡

Real-Time Processing

The processing screen updates live using WebSocket technology — the practitioner sees each analysis stage complete in real time without reloading the page. No waiting, no uncertainty.

🤖

AI Doctor Avatar

An animated AI video assistant that explains analysis results and treatment recommendations in plain, personable language — both for practitioners reviewing results and patients viewing their score. Brings the data to life in a human way.

💊

Treatment Plan System

AI-generated, fully editable treatment plans with clinical and patient-friendly naming, priority ranking, skincare protocols, and practitioner notes. Plans can be saved as reusable clinic templates for efficiency.

🔗

Secure Report Sharing

Practitioners share results via a time-limited secure link — no patient account required. Patients view a beautifully designed, plain-language version of their results. Consent is collected digitally before any data is displayed.

📈

Progress Tracking

Every scan is stored so patients and practitioners can see how collagen health changes over time. Multi-line charts show overall and per-layer progress, with before/after comparisons and treatment effectiveness bands.

5. Security & Compliance

The platform is designed from the ground up to meet GDPR (Europe), HIPAA (US), and UAE health data regulations. Security is not added on top — it is built into every layer of the database architecture.

Protection How It Works Regulation Met
Data Isolation Every database record is tagged with a clinic_id. Row-Level Security (RLS) rules prevent any clinic from ever reading another clinic's data — enforced at the database level, not just the app. HIPAA · GDPR
Consent Records Patient consent is digitally recorded with an exact timestamp, IP address, and consent version before any data is shown. Consent records are never deleted — withdrawals are recorded, not erased. GDPR Art. 6 · UAE
Audit Logs Every action — who viewed a patient, who exported a report, who changed consent — is logged with a timestamp and IP address. Downloadable as CSV from clinic Settings. HIPAA · GDPR Art. 30
Token-Based Sharing Patient report links use a unique random token that expires in 24 hours. No patient password is required to view results — and no unauthorized party can guess the link. GDPR · Best Practice
Encryption All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Patient images stored in cloud storage have matching access policies — only the owning clinic can retrieve them. HIPAA · UAE
Role-Based Access Admin, Practitioner, and Patient roles each see only the data appropriate to their role. Patients never see clinical values. Practitioners never see other clinics' data. Admins never see individual patient health records. HIPAA Minimum Necessary
MFA for Practitioners Multi-factor authentication is available for all practitioner accounts. When enabled, a 6-digit code from an authenticator app is required at every login. Best Practice
AI Disclaimer All AI analysis screens and exported PDFs carry a mandatory disclaimer: results are AI-assisted estimates based on visible skin markers and do not replace medical diagnosis. UAE AI Regulation

6. Scalability Notes

The architecture is designed to grow with the platform. What works for 10 clinics will work for 1,000 — without needing to rebuild.

⚡ Real-time updates via WebSocket — no polling, instant status changes across devices
🧩 Modular layers — the AI model, PDF generator, and notification service can each be upgraded independently
🏥 Multi-tenant from day one — adding a new clinic is a single database record, no new infrastructure needed
📦 Shared database with tenant isolation — cost-efficient at MVP, upgradeable to dedicated databases per clinic for enterprise tier
🤖 AI training layer built in — every practitioner correction improves the model over time (flywheel effect)
📱 Mobile-first with web fallback — the iOS app is the primary experience; web works for image upload and report preview where camera is not required
🔌 Serverless edge functions — PDF generation, email sending, and AI triggers run as serverless functions that scale automatically with load
